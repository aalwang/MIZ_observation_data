# MIZ_observation_data
Zenodo: [![DOI](https://zenodo.org/badge/1211463948.svg)](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20834203)

Contains observational datasets from in and around Arctic marginal ice zones (MIZ). This data is used in the paper "Dependence of Surface Fluxes and Clouds on Sea Ice Concentration Around Arctic Marginal Ice Zones in Observations and ERA5" .
